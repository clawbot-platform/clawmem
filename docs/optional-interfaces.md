# Optional future `clawmem` interfaces from Deliverables A–D

`clawmem` is intentionally optional for the first deterministic scoring releases.

Use `clawmem` later for:
- institution-scale prior-case retrieval
- retrieval of accepted historical examples
- contradiction-pattern similarity retrieval
- graph-context retrieval beyond direct same-pair history
- multi-hop relationship retrieval
- example-memory retrieval for analyst-note quality
- institutional memory beyond direct same-pair or direct OFAC relationship evidence

Do not make `clawmem` a hard dependency until:
- direct same-pair history scoring is stable
- OFAC relationship scoring is stable
- promotion coverage is expanded
- `claw-identity` evidence contracts are stable
